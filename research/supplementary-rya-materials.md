**Access date:** 2026-03-15
**Publisher:** Royal Yachting Association (RYA)
**Content summary:**
Official RYA course landing page. Confirms course duration (40 hours + exam), delivery formats (classroom, online, distance learning), and the canonical nine syllabus topics:

1. Seamanship
2. Coastal navigation and pilotage
3. Chartwork
4. Electronic charts
5. Position fixing
6. Plotting a course to steer
7. Weather forecasting and meteorology

## SUP-007 — Sailing Course Online: RYA Day Skipper Theory Online

**URL:** https://www.sailingcourseonline.co.uk/rya-day-skipper-theory-online/
**Access date:** 2026-03-15
**Publisher:** Sailing Course Online (RYA Recognised Training Centre, Hamble Point Marina)
**Content summary:**
Detailed course description page. Provides a 7-module breakdown used in their online delivery:

1. Navigation & Chartwork (position fixing, course calculations, passage planning)
2. Tides & Tidal Streams (height calcs, tidal diamonds, secondary port corrections)
3. Meteorology (shipping forecasts, pressure systems, fronts)
4. Collision Regulations (IRPCS steering rules, nav lights, sound signals)
5. Pilotage (IALA buoyage, cardinal/lateral marks, harbour entry)
6. Electronic Navigation (chartplotters, GPS, radar)
7. Safety & Seamanship (distress, DSC, VHF, MOB)

Also describes exam structure: 2 papers × 120 minutes (General + Chartwork), open book using RYA Training Almanac and Charts 3 & 4.
