## SUP-014 — Skippers Online: RYA Theory Exam Guidance

**URL:** https://www.skippersonline.net/exam-guidance/
**Access date:** 2026-03-15
**Organisation:** Skippers Online (RYA-recognised online training provider)
**Type:** Free web page — exam structure reference

**Content summary:**
Clear breakdown of the Day Skipper Theory exam format:
- 2 papers × 90 minutes each
- Paper 1: General Questions (theory)
- Paper 2: Chartwork (open book — RYA Training Almanac + Charts 3 & 4 only)
- Pass criterion: "sufficient understanding of the subject, even if answers contain some errors"

Key assessed topic areas: fixes and estimated positions, course to steer, tidal heights, tidal set/drift, Collision Regulations.

Also covers Yachtmaster and Ocean Yachtmaster exam structures for comparison.

## SUP-015 — Nomad Sailing: Day Shapes Self Test

**URL:** https://www.nomadsailing.co.uk/learning-zone/day-shapes-test.html
**Access date:** 2026-03-15
**Organisation:** Nomad Sailing Ltd (RYA training centre)
**Type:** Free interactive web quiz — image-recognition format

**Content summary:**
Interactive flashcard-style self-test covering all 13 vessel day shapes assessed in the RYA Day Skipper and Yachtmaster Theory exams. Hover-to-reveal format mirrors exam presentation style. Covers: RAM, Fishing, Divers, Aground, Anchor, Constrained by Draught, Fishing >150m gear, Mine-sweeping, Motorsailing, Not Under Command, Pilot, RAM with hazard, Towing >200m.

**Notes:** Excellent flashcard candidate source for the Day Shapes topic within COLREGS.

**Content summary:**

## SUP-023 — UK Government: IRPCS Full Text (MSN 1781)

**URL:** https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/281965/msn1781.pdf
**Access date:** 2026-03-15
**Organisation:** Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA) / UK Government
**Type:** Free official PDF — full IRPCS text

**Content summary:**
Complete text of the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea (COLREGS / IRPCS) as incorporated into UK law via Marine Guidance Note MSN 1781. Authoritative reference for all Rules, Annexes, lights and shapes specifications. Free public access.
