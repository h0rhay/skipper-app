# Page 7 — Mooring Alongside a Wall and in a Raft

## Visible Headings
- MOORING ALONGSIDE
- ALONGSIDE A WALL
- In a raft

## Content

### ALONGSIDE A WALL

Be aware of the rise and fall of tide.

Use stretchy nylon warps.

round turn and two half hitches

Run lines through a fairlead and then onto a cleat.

Use separate lines for each different task.

When you have attached a warp to a bollard/ring etc, bring the rest of the line back on board and attach it to a cleat — each warp can then be independently adjusted from on board as the tide rises and falls.

Length of warps should be at least 4 x the rise and fall of tide.

### In a raft

Attach breast ropes and springs between vessels.

Attach breast ropes between vessels at bow and stern.

bow shore line

stern shore line

Outside boat should take bow and stern lines ashore to minimise strain on the inside boat's shore lines.
