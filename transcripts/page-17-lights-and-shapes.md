## Diagrams & Illustrations

Bottom half (LIGHTS AND SHAPES — basic vessels):
- Sailing vessel under sail less than 20m: Three views of a sailing yacht. First showing a tricolour masthead light. Second (OR option) showing an afaft-the-beam arrangement with stern light and bicolour light at bow. Labels: "Tricolour light", "OR", "Afaft the beam", "Stern light", "Bicolour".
- Power-driven vessels: Multiple views. A small power vessel showing: "Steaming light" (white forward-facing), "Bicolour" (red/green at bow), "Stern light" (white aft). A separate illustration showing steaming light and bicolour arrangement from the side. Red X symbol on a vessel with NEVER caption.
- Sailing vessel under power diagram showing a vessel by day with a black cone (inverted) — labelled "motoring cone" and "By day".
- Sector diagram showing light arcs for a power-driven vessel: four sectors with degree measurements — 225°, 112.5°, 112.5°, 22.5° — shown on an overhead plan view. Labels: "Starboard view", "From astern", "From ahead", "Port view". Views illustrated as seen from each direction.
- Larger ships with two steaming lights: illustration showing a ship with two white steaming lights at different heights forward.

Top half (LIGHTS AND SHAPES — special vessels):
Each vessel type shown in a horizontal band with a small black silhouette day shape and an illuminated night-light arrangement. Bands from left to right:

- At anchor: black ball day shape, single all-round white light at night.
- Restricted in ability to manoeuvre (eg. large container ships or fisheries in a narrow channel): three all-round lights (red-white-red vertical arrangement) by night; three black balls/shapes by day. Caption: "eg. large container ships or fisheries in a narrow channel — 3 all round reds [PARTIALLY OBSCURED]"
- Constrained by draught: black cylinder day shape; lights shown.
- Carrying out underwater work (eg. dredging, cable laying, towing): day shapes shown; night lights shown.
- Fishing trawling: two cones apex-together day shape; lights.
- Other types of fishing: caption "white lights have same sector as steaming light", "from astern" label; day shape shown.
- Towing: diamond shape over 200m day shape; night lights shown with additional masthead light for over 200m.
- Air cushion vessel: yellow all-round light, day shape shown.
- On pilot duty: by day — flag H (vertical halves, white and red); night lights shown; 1m label.
- Diving: by day — flag A (white and blue swallowtail); night lights shown.
- Minesweeping: three green lights / shapes shown.

## Transcription Notes

The top half of the page shows vessels in a series of dark-background bands, each with day shapes and night light arrangements. Some text captions for individual vessel bands are very small; all legible text has been captured. The degree values on the sector diagram (225°, 112.5°, 22.5°) are as printed. "Afaft" appears as printed in the image — likely intended as "Abaft".
