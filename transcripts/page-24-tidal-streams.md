### TIDAL STREAMS

#### Consider the tide as a travelator

Go against the flow – travel slowly
Go with the flow – travel quickly

If you travel across the tide the boat will be pushed sideways giving a different ground track to the course you are steering.

[Diagram labels:]
- Heading
- Ground track
- tide

The direction and strength of tidal streams depends on:
- your location
- whether it is springs, neaps or in between
- the time relative to high water at a reference port.
