## Content

### IALA – MARITIME BUOYAGE SYSTEM
Two buoyage systems exist in the world, IALA A and IALA B. The difference affects the colour and light characteristics of lateral marks.
IALA A is used in Europe, Africa, Russia, India, Australia and New Zealand.
IALA B, which is illustrated on page 62, is used in the USA, South America, the parts of the Caribbean, South East Asia and Canada.
