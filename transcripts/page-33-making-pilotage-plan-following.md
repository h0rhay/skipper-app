### Following Your Plan — six illustrated techniques (top half)
1. Transit: illustration of vessel with two marks in line ahead
2. Contour: echo sounder/depth gauge showing "90" with "closer" label, depth contour at "5m" shown as seabed profile
3. Clearance over bar: open almanac/tide table showing tidal calculations
4. Positive identification of marks: arrow pointing to IALA A buoyage marks on a chart (red and green marks visible), label "IALA A buoyage"
5. Back bearing: hands holding a hand bearing compass, using it over stern
6. Clearing lines: two headlands or hazards with "Danger" labels, safe corridor shown between clearing lines
